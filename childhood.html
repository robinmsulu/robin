Childhood Experiences

Memorable Moments

During my childhood, I had the opportunity to build my first computer game. It all started when I discovered a programming book at the local library. The book introduced me to the world of coding, and I was fascinated by the idea of creating my own interactive experience.

Technical Skills Developed

Even in my early years, I showed an interest in technology. I started developing basic computer skills and explored programming languages like Scratch and Logo.

Hobbies

One of my favorite hobbies during childhood was drawing and painting. Whether it was sketching in my sketchbook, experimenting with watercolors, or creating colorful masterpieces with crayons, I found joy and inspiration in the world of visual arts.

Creative Talents

My creative side began to blossom as I discovered a passion for storytelling and writing. Whether it was crafting imaginative tales, creating fictional worlds, or jotting down my thoughts in a journal, I enjoyed expressing myself through the written word].
